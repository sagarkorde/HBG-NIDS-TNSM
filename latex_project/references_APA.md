## Notes on APA Formatting

- Journal titles are italicised; volume numbers are italicised; issue numbers are
  in parentheses (not italicised).
- DOI/URL included where available.
- "et al." used for works with 3+ authors per APA 7th ed.
- Conference proceedings use the format: Author(s). (Year). *Title*. In *Proceedings
  Title* (pp. xx–xx). Publisher.
- Foundational references (Benford 1938, Page 1954, Roberts 1959, Liu 2008,
  Siffer 2017) cited at original publication per standard academic convention.
